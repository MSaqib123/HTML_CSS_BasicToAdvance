1. text-align — left | center | right | justify
  2. text-indent — first line indent (2em, 20px…)
  3. text-transform — uppercase | lowercase | capitalize | none
  4. white-space — normal | nowrap | pre-wrap | pre-line
  5. word-spacing — space between words (0.1em, 2px…)
  6. letter-spacing — space between letters / tracking (0.05em, -0.02em…)
  7. text-overflow — ellipsis (needs overflow:hidden + white-space:nowrap)
  8. overflow-wrap — break-word | anywhere (prevents long word overflow)
  9. word-break — normal | break-all (especially for long URLs or codes)
  10. hyphens — auto (automatic hyphenation in justified text)
  11. direction — ltr | rtl (important for multilingual sites)
  12. vertical-align — baseline | middle | top | sub | super (for inline elements)
